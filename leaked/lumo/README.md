  [Pivot to AI](https://pivot-to-ai.com/2025/08/02/protons-lumo-ai-chatbot-not-end-to-end-encrypted-not-open-source/)
  and the [European Open Source AI Index](https://osai-index.eu/news/lumo-proton-least-open/)
  for background. Corrected here after initially being scoped as a
  candidate open-source add.
